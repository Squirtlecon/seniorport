Xi Xing Ping Fighting Game

A Street Fighter inspired fighting game. It was intended to have various world leaders and political figures, but only Xi Xing Ping was implemented. Built using the Godot Engine in GDScript.

Background

During my Junior year, me and a friend had an assigment to make a game. We decided on a street fighter inspired game, with world leaders. We primarily did this due to finding it hilarous, along with making it have a bit of political satire.

The main goal of the game was to get the basic fighting working working. This was our first time using Godot, so we also focused on learning the programming language.


The Team

Me and my friend Isaac Zhang decided to team up. We become friends in 3rd grade, and have worked together in multiple app academy projects. We have also worked on many hobby and school projects including things such as Minecraft maps and group projects.








My Role

I was sent to be the primary sprite artist, and worked on the UI. I coded in the UI and made all the textures and animations.



The UI

While creating the UI, Isaac had requested the stats of defense, hp, and ultimate progress. Defense would be used for shielding damage, hp for taking damage and ultimate progess to show when you can use it.
